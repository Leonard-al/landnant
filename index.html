Request Data Deletion

Follow these steps to request account deletion:

  1. Enter your email address below and submit the request.
  2. If an account with that email exists, we will send a confirmation link.
  3. Click the link in the email to confirm and complete the deletion process.

Data Deletion Policy

Upon confirmation, all personal data, including any properties associated with your account, will be permanently deleted. However, some data may be retained for legal or compliance purposes for a limited period.
